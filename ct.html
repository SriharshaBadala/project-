Traditional festival foods of Chhattisgarh

Baster Dussehra

The traditional festival of Chhattisgarh is called "Bastar Dussehra."It is a vibrant and grand celebration that takes place in the Bastar region of Chhattisgarh. During this festival, the local tribes and communities come together to worship Devi Maoli, the reigning deity of Bastar.The festival lasts for around 75 days and involves various rituals, cultural performances, music, dance, and processions. It is a unique and colorful celebration that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Chhattisgarh.This festival is known for its unique rituals and cultural extravaganza.The locals beautifully decorate their homes and streets, and the entire region comes alive with vibrant colors and joyful celebrations. It's a wonderful time to experience the rich cultural heritage of Chhattisgarh and witness the unique traditions of Bastar Dussehra..
