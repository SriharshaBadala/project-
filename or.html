Chenna Poda

The Chenna Poda, cooked for several hours, is a festival-special as it is believed to be Lord Jagannath's favourite sweet dish. The sweetness of jaggery, the tang of chenna and the aroma of burnt caramelised sugar gives this dessert a distinct flavour that has put Odisha on the map of Indian culinary traditions.
